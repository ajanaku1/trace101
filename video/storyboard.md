# Video Storyboard: "Your Wallet Has a Shadow"
## Scene-by-Scene Visual Guide

---

## SCENE 1: The Address (0:00 - 0:08)
**Duration:** 8 seconds

**Visual:**
- Pure black screen for 2 seconds
- A single wallet address fades in, glowing purple: `7xKp...3mNq`
- Subtle pulse animation on the address
- Small particles floating around it

**Camera:** Static, centered

**Audio:** Low ambient drone begins

**Text overlay:** None

**Prompt for AI video tools:**
> "Single glowing purple text string floating in pure black void, subtle particle effects, cinematic, minimal, 4K"

---

## SCENE 2: The Web Grows (0:08 - 0:25)
**Duration:** 17 seconds

**Visual:**
- From the wallet address, thin purple lines start extending outward
- Each line connects to a smaller node (other wallets)
- Labels fade in next to nodes: "Exchange," "DEX Trade," "Friend," "NFT Purchase"
- The network continues expanding, becoming increasingly complex
- By the end, the screen is filled with an intricate web

**Camera:** Slow zoom out to reveal scale

**Audio:** Tension builds, subtle electronic pulses with each new connection

**Prompt for AI video tools:**
> "Network graph visualization growing organically, purple glowing nodes and connections on black background, data visualization aesthetic, connections spreading like neural network"

---

## SCENE 3: The Profile (0:25 - 0:40)
**Duration:** 15 seconds

**Visual:**
- The web consolidates to one side
- A "profile card" slides in from the right:
  ```
  ┌─────────────────────────────┐
  │  [Silhouette Icon]          │
  │                             │
  │  Active: 9am-11pm EST       │
  │  Estimated Value: $XXX,XXX  │
  │  Risk Score: HIGH           │
  │  Connected Wallets: 12      │
  │  Trading Style: Aggressive  │
  └─────────────────────────────┘
  ```
- Each line item appears sequentially
- Red warning indicator pulses on "Risk Score"

**Camera:** Slight push toward profile card

**Audio:** Each reveal has a soft "data ping" sound

**Prompt for AI video tools:**
> "Futuristic data profile card hologram, dark interface, purple and red accents, surveillance aesthetic, minimal UI design floating in space"

---

## SCENE 4: The Threats (0:40 - 1:10)
**Duration:** 30 seconds

**Visual:** Three mini-scenes, 10 seconds each

### 4A: Front-Running
- Show a simple trade arrow going from Wallet A to DEX
- A bot icon appears, zooms ahead of the arrow
- Bot's trade completes first, price indicator goes up
- Original trade completes at worse price

### 4B: Targeting
- Wallet with visible balance ($500K glowing)
- Crosshairs/target overlay appears on it
- Phishing hooks and warning icons surround it

### 4C: Copy Trading
- Original wallet makes a trade
- Multiple "copy" wallets appear, mirroring the same action
- Original wallet's edge/alpha literally drains away (visualized as color fading)

**Camera:** Quick cuts between scenarios

**Audio:** More urgent pacing, staccato electronic beats

---

## SCENE 5: The Shield (1:10 - 1:35)
**Duration:** 25 seconds

**Visual:**
- Return to the complex surveillance web from Scene 2
- A shield icon (matching Trace brand) materializes in center
- The shield pulses with purple energy
- Selective connections begin breaking—not all, just specific ones
- The breaking connections dissolve into particles
- What remains: a cleaner graph with intentional gaps

**Camera:** Centered, slight zoom on shield during pulse

**Audio:** Tension releases, more hopeful tone, resolution chord

**Prompt for AI video tools:**
> "Glowing purple shield icon breaking network connections, protective energy pulse, connections dissolving into particles, cybersecurity visualization, hopeful mood"

---

## SCENE 6: encrypt.trade Demo (1:35 - 2:00)
**Duration:** 25 seconds

**Visual:**
- Simple UI mockup of encrypt.trade
- Show a trade being initiated
- Instead of broadcasting to visible mempool (shown as exposed), trade goes through encrypted channel (shown as shielded tunnel)
- Trade arrives at destination without the surveillance web detecting it
- Checkmark appears: "Trade executed privately"

**Camera:** Clean UI transitions

**Audio:** Confident, resolved tone

**Prompt for AI video tools:**
> "Clean minimal crypto trading interface, transaction going through glowing protected tunnel, dark theme purple accents, successful completion animation"

---

## SCENE 7: Call to Action (2:00 - 2:30)
**Duration:** 30 seconds

**Visual:**
- Trace logo animates in (the signature lines-with-nodes mark)
- Text: "See Your Exposure"
- URL: trace-wheat.vercel.app
- Transition to encrypt.trade logo
- Text: "Trade Privately"
- URL: encrypt.trade
- Final frame: Both logos side by side
- Tagline: "Your money. Your trades. Your privacy."

**Camera:** Static, bold typography focus

**Audio:** Resolves to calm, confident ending. Music fades.

## Tools Recommendations

**For AI Generation:**
- Runway Gen-3 (best for abstract motion graphics)
- Pika Labs (good for quick iterations)
- Kling AI (alternative option)

**For Professional Production:**
- After Effects + Particular for particle effects
- Cinema 4D for 3D node network visualization
- Figma/Principle for UI animations

**For Voiceover:**
- Natural human voice preferred (not AI)
- Male or female, calm and authoritative
- Avoid "tech bro" or "marketing hype" energy
